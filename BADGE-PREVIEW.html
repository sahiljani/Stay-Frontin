How to Enable These Badges

For HOT PRODUCT badge:

Add the tag hot or hot-product to your product in Shopify admin.

For LOW STOCK badge:

Automatically shows when inventory is ≤ 10 items (configurable).

For SAVE % badge:

Set a "Compare at price" higher than the regular price in Shopify admin. The percentage is calculated automatically.
